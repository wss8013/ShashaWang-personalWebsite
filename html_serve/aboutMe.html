Why Computer Science
by Shasha Wang

Portfolio

I received my previous master degree in Professional Accounting at Macquarie University in Australia. As an accountant, I started using an online software that helps people do their taxes and I have also used it for a few years to file our family tax returns. I was surprised by how amazing the job was done by the software. It takes much less time than a real CPA and does it far more accurately. I was amazed by how computer software can help people live a much simpler life. The potential of computing is endless. So I decided to pursue a degree in Computer Science and try to do some impactful work to help people around the world.My goal is to complete my master degree in computer science, gain the essential skills and find an opportunity to pursue a career as a software engineer.
